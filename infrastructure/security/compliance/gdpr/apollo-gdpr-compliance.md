# GDPR Operational Checklist

- Data Processing Agreements signed with every surveillance data supplier.
- Data Subject Requests automated through the Apollo portal; SLA 30 days.
- Privacy Impact Assessments stored in this folder and referenced by ticket number.
- Default retention: 5 years for investigations, 2 years for biometric telemetry unless legal hold.
- Dedicated DPO contact: dpo@apollo.local.
